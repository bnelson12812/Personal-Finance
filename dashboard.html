Each month, upload fresh CSV exports from your bank to GitHub:

  1. Export your debit and credit CSVs from your bank's website
  2. Go to your GitHub repository ‚Üí

  4. Upload as debit.csv and credit.csv
  5. Click Commit changes
  6. Come back here and click Refresh data in the banner above
